Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who can participate?

All college students with valid ID cards can participate in the events.

2. Is there a registration fee?

Most events are free. Some competitions may have a nominal fee of Rs. 50-100.

3. Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can register for multiple events as long as the timings don't clash.

4. What should I bring?

Bring your college ID, registration confirmation, and any materials needed for your events.

5. Are there prizes?

Yes! Winners receive certificates, trophies, and cash prizes worth over Rs. 1 lakh.

6. Is accommodation available?

Limited accommodation is available for outstation participants. Contact us for details.
